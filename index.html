NFT

As the first ever NFT-funded documentary, Isolated America is a collaborative project between filmmakers and photographers from across the nation. Since March 2020 our team has been investigating how the coronavirus crisis has exacerbated harsh realities faced by everyday Americans. Filming coast to coast in eleven states, we have been working to share the personal struggles of essential workers, community organizers, nurses, teachers, unhoused individuals, religious leaders, at-risk populations and mutual aid organizations.

We are very excited to be the first NFT in the documentary film industry and are seeking support from the crypto community to help us share these important stories. These NFT offerings will provide the funding needed for follow-up interviews, licensing fees, color correction, post production and distribution. We encourage NFT investors to see themselves as part of the production team by collaborating and advising on the three main subjects of the film: community, healthcare, and the economy. Holders of executive producer NFTs will also receive dividends from the filmâ€™s distribution.

We believe acquiring funding through an NFT offering would give us the creative flexibility and collaborative support needed to allow our audiences to decide what they see in the media for themselves. Be part of making history as you help create a paradigm shift in how films and media are created.

Feel free to reach out with any questions: contact@isolatedamerica.com

Mission



This film works to reveal institutionalized inequalities and how they contributed to the lack of support for Americans during the coronavirus pandemic. Focusing on small business owners, nurses, civil rights leaders, support volunteers, migrant workers, undocumented families, immunocompromised individuals, mutual aid groups, reform-minded politicians, members of the Navajo nation, healthcare employees, business owners, Amazon employees, concerned citizens and so many others. We are examining the systemic issues that have made so many vulnerable to this illness. Exploring why certain groups are being particularly affected in these times.

Isolated America aims to capture this historic moment,
for us as individuals and our nation.
