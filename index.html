Trailer

We are American workers:
nurses, miners, teachers, hairstylists, and delivery drivers. We stock warehouses, maintain the food supply, and provide hospitality. We cook, clean, and manufacture. We provide personal services. We're in construction, agriculture, and maintenance. Coronavirus changed everything for us, and we need you to listen.

This is our story.

Mission

Our film works to reveal institutionalized inequalities and how they contributed to the lack of support for Americans during the coronavirus pandemic. Focusing on small business owners, nurses, support volunteers, migrant workers, undocumented families, immunocompromised individuals, mutual aid groups, reform-minded politicians, members of the Navajo nation, healthcare employees, business owners, Amazon employees, concerned citizens and so many others, we will examine the systemic issues that have made so many vulnerable to this illness.

Isolated America aims to capture this historic moment for us as individuals and as a nation.

Team

We are a team of 3 filmmakers taking a dramatic, firsthand look at our nation during the coronavirus outbreak. We will split into teams and travel across the country as we document, interview and experience this historical event.

Contact

We are looking for hardworking people to tell America how they've been impacted by coronavirus. If you would like to share your story, please contact contact@isolatedamerica.com or call (520) 261-6763 to leave a message describing your personal experience during the pandemic.
