NFT

As the first ever NFT-funded documentary, Isolated America is a collaborative project between filmmakers and photographers from across the nation. Since March 2020 our team has been investigating how the coronavirus crisis has exacerbated harsh realities faced by everyday Americans. Filming coast to coast in eleven states, we have been working to capture the struggles of essential workers, community organizers, healthcare workers, teachers, unhoused individuals, at-risk populations and mutual aid organizations.

We are very excited to be the first NFT offering in the documentary film space and to be collaborating with the crypto community to help us share these important stories. These tokens will provide the vital funding needed for post production, follow-up interviews, licensing fees and distribution cost. We encourage NFT holders to see themselves as part of the production team.

We will be offering tokens at three levels: supporter, investor and executive producer.

  • Supporters will be credited.
  • Investors can collaborate directly with our team.
  • Executive producers will receive dividens from the film's distribution.
We believe seeking funding through an NFT offering will provide us creative flexibility and collaborative support from the community. Allowing the audience to voice what they what in the documentary. Be part of making history as you help create a paradigm shift in how films and media are created.

Mission



This film works to reveal institutionalized inequalities and how they contributed to the lack of support for Americans during the coronavirus pandemic. Focusing on small business owners, nurses, civil rights leaders, support volunteers, migrant workers, undocumented families, immunocompromised individuals, mutual aid groups, reform-minded politicians, members of the Navajo nation, healthcare employees, business owners, Amazon employees, concerned citizens and so many others. We are examining the systemic issues that have made so many vulnerable to this illness. Exploring why certain groups are being particularly affected in these times.


Three main tenets of the film: community, healthcare, and the economy.

Isolated America aims to capture this historic moment,
for us as individuals and our nation.
